Our Story

Redefining Sustainability Through Innovation

CarbonScope is an ELP project developed by B.Tech CSE (AI & ML) students, aimed at promoting eco-conscious living through technology. It helps users track and reduce their carbon footprint effortlessly, combining AI-powered analysis with gamified engagement to make sustainability simple, measurable, and impactful.
